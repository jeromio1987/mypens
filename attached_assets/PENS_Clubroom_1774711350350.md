# PENS — Clubroom Spec

## What It Is
The narrative and identity layer of PENS. Not a dashboard. Not a data view. The place where behaviour becomes meaning and data becomes story.

## Tone
- Masculine but intelligent
- Real-life, not biohacker fantasy
- Slightly editorial — like a lifestyle magazine with data
- "Continental collective" vibe
- Never preachy. Never guilty. Never performative.

---

## Formats

### Daily Card
Short. One headline, one supporting line, one data point if available.

**Structure:**
```
[Headline — what happened]
[Context — why it makes sense]
[Number or stat — optional]
```

**Examples:**

Locked In day:
> "Ate clean, slept well, showed up."
> Garmin confirmed: 7h12 sleep, HRV above baseline.

Balanced — social day:
> "Controlled damage. Worth it."
> +1.2kg on the scale — water, not fat. Back to baseline in 2 days.

Balanced — normal day:
> "Nothing remarkable. That's the point."
> Consistent week. 7-day trend: stable.

Off — chaos day:
> "Full send. No apologies."
> System is tracking. Expect +1–3kg (water). Temporary.

Off — recovery day:
> "The body is doing the work."
> Sleep score: 84. HRV recovering. Don't train today.

---

### Weekly Card (Hero Format)
The most important format. This is what users come back for.

**Structure:**
```
[Week summary headline]
[Mode distribution — visual or text]
[Key insight — one line]
[One honest observation — no spin]
```

**Example:**
> "A week that looked messy but held."
> 3 days Balanced / 2 days Social / 1 day Off / 1 day Recovery
> Weight: net +0.4kg vs last week — within normal range.
> Sleep dipped Wednesday. Garmin noticed. You probably did too.

---

## Card Logic Rules

1. Cards are generated from available data — never faked
2. If data is insufficient, no card is shown (silence > generic filler)
3. Never show a card that could shame the user
4. Off mode cards never apologise for the behaviour — they contextualise it
5. Weekly card always shows mode distribution to build self-awareness over time

---

## Phase 1 — Private Only
No sharing. No social. Just the user and their own story. This is the foundation.

Cards available:
- Daily card (requires: mode + at least one data point)
- Weekly card (requires: 5+ days of data)

Cards NOT available yet:
- Insight patterns ("you always sleep badly after…")
- Predictive cards ("based on your history…")
- Medals / achievements

---

## Phase 2 — Shareable Cards
Export a card as an image. Clean, aesthetic, minimal.
Share to WhatsApp, IG, or privately to Inner Circle.

Format requirements:
- No raw data dump
- No app branding overload
- Reads well as a standalone image
- Optional: user name or initials only

## Naming & Language Bank (seed list)

Phrases that fit the tone:
- "Controlled damage"
- "Full send"
- "The body kept score"
- "Nothing remarkable. That's the point."
- "System normalising"
- "Back in range"
- "Held the line"
- "Expected. Not a problem."
- "The data says recovery. Listen."
- "Week in perspective"

Phrases to avoid:
- "You failed to…"
- "You should have…"
- "Warning"
- "Off track"
- "Cheat"
- "Bad day"
- "You didn't meet your goal"

## Build Notes for Replit
Daily card = string template with variable slots. Mode + data points → select template → fill slots. Weekly card = aggregate query (7 days of mode + weight + sleep) → narrative template. 3 modes × 2 sub-contexts (Off-chaos / Off-recovery, Balanced-social / Balanced-normal) = ~5 template variants to start. Do not attempt dynamic sentence generation in Phase 1.
